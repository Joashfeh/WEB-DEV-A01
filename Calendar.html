About the game

Calendar is a turn-based roguelike dungeon game made by a team of 4 for my end-of-semester Studio Project. The game is a full ASCII art game run in the windows console.
The theme given to us was '2020', thus we decided to base our game around how 2020 gets worse progressively.

To complete the game, the player must proceed through procedurally generated levels and fight every enemy on the floor to proceed to the next floor. There is a chance that gold will spawn on each floor, to better equip the players with items to fight enemies on the higher floors.
